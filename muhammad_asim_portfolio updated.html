About Me

I'm Muhammad Asim, a driven student and multi-faceted professional from Taxila, Pakistan. Currently pursuing a BS in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Haripur, I bring a strong technical foundation from my DAE in Mechatronics at GCT Taxila.

DAE Final Year Project

Featured Project

🏆 Exhibition Award — 2024

PaintBot

⚙️ Mechatronics Department · GCT Taxila

An autonomous wall-painting robot designed and built as a DAE Final Year Project. PaintBot navigates along walls automatically, sprays paint uniformly, and operates without manual human labor — solving a real-world problem in the construction and interior industry. The robot features an intelligent control system, motorized wheel drive, an onboard paint sprayer, and real-time LCD status display.
